Questions answered

Things people usually ask us

How much does a website cost? +
Our websites start from £799 for a simple, professional site. More complex projects like online shops or larger sites are priced individually. We'll always give you a clear, fixed quote before any work begins — no nasty surprises.
How long does it take? +
Most websites are designed and ready for your feedback within 2 weeks. From start to launch, the typical timeline is 3–5 weeks. We'll give you a clear schedule at the start of your project.
Do I need any technical knowledge? +
Not at all. We handle every technical part — you just need to tell us about your business. We explain everything in plain English, and we'll also show you how to make simple updates yourself if you want to.
Will my website show up on Google? +
Yes — every site we build is set up with the basics of SEO (search engine optimisation) included. That means Google can find and index your site straight away. For businesses wanting to rank higher for competitive searches, we also offer additional SEO packages.
What happens after my site goes live? +
All our websites come with 30 days of free support after launch. After that, we offer affordable monthly maintenance plans so your site stays updated and secure. Or you can manage it yourself — we'll show you how.
Can I update the website myself? +
Absolutely. We build your site so you can easily make changes — update opening hours, add new photos, change prices — without needing to know any code. If you ever get stuck, just give us a call.
